Oman in focus

From showcasing Oman’s contemporary art at the La Biennale di Venezia in Italy to showcasing tourism at the Arabian Travel Market (ATM) 2022 in Dubai, Oman's Ministry of Heritage and Tourism is focused on getting the Sultanate in the global spotlight.

The Ministry of Tourism and Heritage together with the Ministry of Culture presents its first National Pavilion at the prestigious 59th International Art Exhibition - La Biennale di Venezia in Venice, Italy. Highlighting Oman’s contemporary art movement, the initiative aims to have a profound impact for Omani art both locally, and on the world stage.

The Sultanate, represented by the Ministry of Heritage and Tourism and a number of Omani tourism sector institutions and facilities, will participate in the Arabian Travel Market (ATM) 2022 exhibition in Dubai, UAE. The main focus at the show will be on ‘The future of international travel and tourism’.

Oman Tourism Forum 2022

Oman's Ministry of Heritage and Tourism will be hosting the Oman Tourism Forum 2022 at the Dubai World Trade Center (DWTC) during the Arabian Travel Market (ATM) 2022. The forum is a high-level event that aims to gather industry leaders to explore tourism and partnership opportunities in Oman through dialogue, and to explore some of the key areas where companies can be involved. Key speakers include officials from the Sultanate of Oman and a number of speakers from China, Europe, the Middle East and Asia.

Always focused on safety

While travel restrictions have become less stringent, Oman Airports still maintains the highest standards of safety and protection at the terminal building and all security checkpoints to ensure the health and wellbeing of passengers and employees. Safety and peace of mind remains a key priority.
